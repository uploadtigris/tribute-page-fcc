A timeline of Nikola Tesla's Life

  • 1859 - Nikola Tesla is Born in Smiljan, Coatia
  • 1870 - Tesla contracts cholera and nearly faces death
  • 1875 - Tesla Enrolls in College on a military scholarship. Proceeds to be an excellent student, even receiving a letter of commendation from the dean of the Technical Department.
  • 1880 - Moves to Prague to Study Philosophy.
  • 1889 - Began His Career As Chieft Electrical Engineer.
  • 1882 - Tesla begins to work for Thomas Edison.
  • 1885 - Tesla Submits Some Of His First Patents
  • 1887 - Re-imagines The Electric Motor
  • 1890 - Tesla Begins His Work On Wirless Power Transmission
  • 1891 - Tesla Patents The Tesla Coil
  • 1943 - Tesla Passes
